# Investment Themes

## Blue Wave Fiscal Stimulus [4]
Democrat Senate victories in Georgia deliver unified government control. Large fiscal packages (COVID relief, infrastructure, green energy) are now legislatively viable. This is a regime shift that favors cyclicals, financials, clean energy, and industrial companies over pure growth/tech.

---

## Semiconductor Supercycle [5]
Structural undersupply of semiconductors across automotive, consumer electronics, AI, and 5G infrastructure. Leading-edge fab capacity is years away from expansion. Fabless designers and foundries (TSM) benefit from pricing power and sustained demand.
