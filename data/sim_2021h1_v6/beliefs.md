# Investment Beliefs

## Institutional Flow Signals Dominate Oscillators [3]
When ADX > 35 and OBV is rising simultaneously, large institutional accumulation is confirmed — this overrides bearish MACD, overbought RSI, and near-upper-BB warnings. The correct action is hold or accumulate, not exit. Symmetrically, when ADX > 30 and OBV is falling in an established position, institutional distribution is confirmed — this overrides intact fundamentals, bullish MACD, and reasonable RSI. The correct action is exit, not hold. ADX threshold lowered from 45 to 35 for the accumulation signal based on repeated evidence: NUE (ADX=39), UNH (ADX=42), and COST exit (ADX=34 declining with OBV falling) all confirm the principle operates reliably in the 30-45 ADX range. This week: NUE and UNH provide simultaneous portfolio confirmation of the hold side; COST provides confirmation of the exit side in the same review.
