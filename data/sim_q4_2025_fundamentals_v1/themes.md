# Investment Themes

## AI Infrastructure Buildout [5]
The AI compute cycle is in full swing. Hyperscalers are accelerating capex on GPU clusters, networking, and inference infrastructure. NVDA, TSM, MU, and PLTR are the core beneficiaries. Apple's pivot to AI glasses (abandoning Vision Pro revamp) further confirms AI-first hardware is the next platform cycle.

---

## Healthcare Biotech Innovation [5]
Aging demographics, GLP-1 drug demand, oncology breakthroughs, and FDA fast-tracking of novel therapeutics are creating a multi-year tailwind for biotech. LLY, REGN, ABBV, and VRTX are the high-conviction names with strong revenue growth and margins.

---

## Clean Energy & Power Infrastructure [3]
Data center power demand from AI is creating insatiable electricity needs. Nuclear (CEG), solar (FSLR), and regulated utilities (NEE) are all benefiting. This is not purely an ESG story — it is an infrastructure capacity story driven by AI workloads.

---

## Legacy Media & Telecom Structural Decline [5]
Cable television, legacy broadcast, and wireline telecoms are in secular decline. Cord-cutting accelerates, streaming fragments revenue, and AI-generated content threatens traditional media moats. CMCSA, T, PARA, DIS, and NFLX (in bear phase) are natural short candidates.
