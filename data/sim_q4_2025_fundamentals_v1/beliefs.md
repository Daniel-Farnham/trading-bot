# Investment Beliefs

## ADX Is the Master Contextualizer for All Technical Signals [3]
ADX > 35 confirms institutional commitment to a directional move and overrides misleading counter-signals in both directions. For longs: don't exit because MACD turns bearish — if OBV is rising AND ADX > 35, institutions are buying the dip and the trend will reassert (Lesson 5). For shorts: don't hold because RSI hits 30 — if ADX > 35, the institutional downtrend is real and sustained (Lesson 3). Critically, the inverse also applies: when a short position's ADX falls toward or below 35 while MACD turns bullish, the institutional conviction supporting the short is eroding — this is an early exit warning even before full Lesson 6 trigger is met (Lessons 6, 11). ADX is the single most important contextualizer before acting on any RSI or MACD signal.
