# Investment Themes

## Consumer Downgrade [5]
Tariff-driven price increases on imported goods will squeeze consumer budgets, accelerating the shift from premium to value retail. Dollar stores (DG), warehouse clubs (COST, WMT), and fast food (MCD) are structural beneficiaries of consumer downtrading.

---

## AI Infrastructure — Sovereign Capital Buildout [5]
Nation-states are deploying sovereign capital into AI infrastructure at unprecedented scale. Saudi Arabia's $1T AI push naming NVDA, AMZN, and PLTR as top beneficiaries — combined with Trump easing AI chip export rules — signals a geopolitical AI arms race where US companies are the primary infrastructure suppliers. Sovereign wealth funds and national AI programs represent a structural demand floor beneath hyperscaler capex that is durable regardless of US corporate earnings cycles.

---

## AI Power Infrastructure [3]
AI data center buildout is consuming electricity at unprecedented and accelerating scale. Elon Musk explicitly stated that after chips, electrical equipment will be AI's next binding constraint. Nuclear and clean power utilities with data center contracts are structural beneficiaries of this constraint — CEG (nuclear, RevGr=+26.1%, RSI=67, ADX=36, OBV rising), NEE, VST. This is a second-order play on AI infrastructure: as chip supply catches up, power becomes the bottleneck, and utilities positioned for data center demand capture that transition. Early theme — score 1/5 — but the founding catalyst is specific and from the most credible source possible.
