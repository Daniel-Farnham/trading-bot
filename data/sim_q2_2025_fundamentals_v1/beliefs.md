# Investment Beliefs

## Disciplined Capital Deployment [3]
Never fully deploy capital in the first review of a new macro regime. Maintain a substantial cash buffer (40%+) in the early weeks to add to winners as theses prove out, capitalize on pullbacks in high-conviction names, and open positions as the macro picture clarifies. The opportunity cost of excess cash is far lower than the cost of full deployment when uncertainty is highest. In a fast-moving macro environment, flexibility is alpha.
