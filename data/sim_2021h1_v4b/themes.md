# Investment Themes

## Blue Wave Reflation [4]
Democrat unified government control unlocks large fiscal stimulus, steeper yield curve, infrastructure spending. Benefits: financials, industrials, commodities, domestic cyclicals. Risk: potential tax increases on corporations and high earners in 2022.

---

## Semiconductor Supercycle [5]
Structural chip shortage driven by 5G, cloud expansion, EV transition, and IoT proliferation. Supply cannot meet demand for 12-18 months minimum. Benefits: TSM, NVDA, MU, AVGO, AMD. TSMC is the irreplaceable foundry.

---

## Digital Finance Revolution [4]
Crypto adoption accelerating with Bitcoin breaking all-time highs, fintech disrupting traditional banking (SQ, SOFI), and digital payments becoming the default. Younger demographics are moving wealth into crypto and fintech platforms.

---

## Healthcare Aging [4]
Structural multi-decade theme driven by aging US and global demographics creating irreversible demand growth across managed care, pharmaceuticals, medical devices, and health services. The US has 10,000 Boomers turning 65 daily through 2030. Key beneficiaries: UNH (dominant Medicare Advantage insurer + Optum services moat), LLY (diabetes/obesity GLP-1 franchise + pipeline optionality), ABBV (immunology/oncology with durable royalty streams), JNJ (diversified healthcare). This theme is rate-regime agnostic and counter-cyclical to our reflation exposure — healthcare spending is non-discretionary. Entry point is confirmed by the strongest technical breadth of any sector this week: UNH ADX=46, LLY RSI=62 MACD bullish, ABBV RSI=61 OBV rising all showing active institutional accumulation.
