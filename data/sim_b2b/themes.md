## Energy Security [5]
Geopolitical realignment in global energy supply chains — Russia-Europe gas disruption, China-Russia axis deepening, and accelerating US LNG export capacity creates a structural multi-year shift in energy trade flows. Nations and corporations are restructuring energy sourcing for security over cost optimization. Covers US LNG exporters, nuclear baseload providers, strategic energy infrastructure, and energy independence technologies.
