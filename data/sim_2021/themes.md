## Commodity/Energy Inflation [4]
Structural commodity price inflation driven by OPEC+ supply discipline holding WTI above $80, global LNG tightness at record spot prices, and post-COVID demand recovery colliding with constrained supply investment. Expressed through integrated oil majors (XOM, CVX) capturing upstream margin expansion, and LNG exporters (LNG/Cheniere) capturing the US-to-international gas price arbitrage. Distinct from the prior 'clean energy transition' angle — this is a supply-constrained commodity pricing theme independent of policy catalysts.
