# Investment Beliefs

## OBV primacy: track institutional flow, not price momentum [3]
In catalyst-driven markets — Fed stress tests, AI chip policy shifts, earnings beats — RSI becomes unreliable as an exit signal because new institutional buyers enter at elevated prices, sustaining overbought readings for weeks. OBV is the authoritative signal for institutional behavior: rising OBV = accumulation regardless of RSI level; falling OBV = distribution regardless of thesis quality. Rule: never exit a position purely on overbought RSI when OBV is rising. Always trigger an exit review when OBV turns negative, even when the fundamental thesis remains intact. A price dip with OBV rising is a buy signal, not an exit.
