# Investment Themes

## AI Enterprise Software Integration [5]
Enterprise companies are embedding AI into core workflows — CRM, CRWD, SNOW are the infrastructure layer. This is distinct from semiconductor AI (NVDA) — it is the software monetization phase of the AI cycle. Data Cloud, security AI, and analytics AI are seeing strong adoption and pricing power.

## Crypto & Digital Asset Recovery [4]
Bitcoin recovering from post-FTX lows with analyst bullish calls re-emerging. COIN showing institutional accumulation (OBV rising, ADX=51). Regulatory clarity potential in 2024 could be a major catalyst. This is an early innings theme — monitoring for confirmation.

---

## Consumer Premiumization vs Commoditization Divergence [2]
Premium consumer brands (CMG, COST, HD, NKE, MCD) are outperforming while commodity-tier retailers (WMT, PG) lag. This reflects the K-shaped recovery: higher-income consumers are still spending freely on experience and quality while lower-income consumers are stretched. Short opportunities may emerge in commodity consumer names.

---

## Healthcare Innovation & GLP-1 Revolution [5]
GLP-1 receptor agonists (Mounjaro, Ozempic, Wegovy, Zepbound) are redefining treatment for obesity, diabetes, and expanding into cardiovascular, addiction, and neurological indications. This is a multi-decade structural shift — over 70% of US adults are overweight or obese, fewer than 3% treated today. LLY and NVO are the primary equity beneficiaries. The addressable market is expanding faster than any pharmaceutical category in modern history, driven by both clinical evidence and demographic aging tailwinds.

---

## Nuclear Renaissance [3]
AI data centers are driving unprecedented electricity demand, pushing hyperscalers toward long-term nuclear power procurement. Microsoft signed a 20-year deal to restart Three Mile Island with Constellation Energy. Amazon is now hiring nuclear energy specialists for its own energy procurement. These deals signal that tech giants are becoming direct counterparties to nuclear generators — providing the long-term revenue visibility nuclear plants need to justify restart and new-build capital. Primary equity beneficiaries: CEG (direct TMI restart), VST (nuclear + gas power fleet), NEE (largest US nuclear operator). This theme is early innings — CEG and VST are currently overbought (RSI=83 and 81 respectively) and require a pullback before entry is viable.

---

## Trump Deregulation & Financial Tailwinds [3]
Trump's second term creates a distinct multi-year macro regime for financials: anticipated rollback of Basel III capital requirements frees bank capital for buybacks and M&A; FTC pivot from aggressive enforcement to permissive dealmaking unlocks M&A premium for financial sector; potential corporate tax cuts expand earnings. Crypto regulatory clarity (SEC leadership change) creates parallel tailwind for COIN and crypto-adjacent names. GS, JPM, SCHW, BLK are all running on this thesis — but all are currently near upper BB (overbought). Theme is real and evidence-backed; positioning must wait for pullbacks.
