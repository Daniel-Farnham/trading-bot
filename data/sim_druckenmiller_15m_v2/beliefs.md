# Investment Beliefs

## Technical Discipline in Institutional Trends [3]
When a stock has confirmed institutional accumulation (ADX>35, OBV rising), use RSI for ENTRY timing only — never for exit. RSI pullbacks to 35-45 in strong trends are accumulation signals. RSI>80 in a strong trend (ADX>35) is NOT a sell signal — it signals strong momentum, not exhaustion. The only valid exit signal is triple distribution: below SMA50 + MACD bearish + OBV falling simultaneously. Critically extended principle from Lesson 7: during macro panic events (efficiency shocks, geopolitical news), OBV is the ONLY signal that matters in the short term — rising OBV during a sector panic means institutions are buying, not selling, regardless of price oscillation or MACD readings. A bearish MACD with rising OBV is a HOLD signal, not a sell signal.

## Confirmed Catalyst Re-Entry [3]
When a major political or regulatory catalyst thesis is confirmed (election result, agency head departure, policy announcement), re-enter stopped-out positions immediately even at higher prices than the original stop. The bulk of the structural move comes AFTER the confirming event, not before. Being stopped out pre-event is disciplined risk management, not thesis invalidation. Trigger: position in Watching status + major catalyst just confirmed + stock is above prior stop price + RSI below 82.
