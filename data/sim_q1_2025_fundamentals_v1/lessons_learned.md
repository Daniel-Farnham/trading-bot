# Lessons Learned

## Lesson 2 [3]
On the first review of a new portfolio, deploy only into the 3-4 highest-conviction ideas. Keep at least 70% cash. The macro picture clarifies over subsequent reviews and the best entries often emerge when a thesis is confirmed by price action, not just news.

---

## Lesson 3 [4]
Short positions require re-evaluation when multiple entry signals simultaneously reverse. If a short was entered on (RSI oversold + ADX>30 downtrend + OBV falling + MACD bearish) and two or more of these signals flip (MACD turns bullish AND OBV turns rising), exit the short regardless of fundamental thesis. Technical reversal of entry conditions = invalidation. The fundamental story and the technical story are independent — you need both to maintain a short.

---

## Lesson 4 [1]
Energy/commodity positions require technical exit discipline even when the macro thesis is intact. When OBV begins falling in an energy name alongside commodity inventory surprises (e.g., crude build 3x above estimate), institutions are front-running fundamental deterioration — exit before waiting for confirmation. The trigger: OBV falling + MACD bearish + below SMA50 in any commodity-linked stock, regardless of the policy thesis backdrop.

---

## Lesson 5 [1]
When a position shows triple distribution (below SMA50 + MACD bearish + OBV falling) AND a respected market participant publicly announces trimming the same stock, treat the combined signal as high-urgency exit requiring same-session action. Professional distribution confirmed by public statements significantly raises the probability of continued near-term downside. Do not wait for the next scheduled review.

---

## Lesson 6 [1]
When a position breaks below its explicit stop price during confirmed triple distribution, positive news catalysts (acquisitions, licensing deals, pipeline announcements) should NOT delay exit. If the market continues distributing a stock through apparently positive news, institutional activity takes precedence over headline catalysts — large holders likely have forward-looking information the public news does not yet reflect. Trigger: stop breach + triple distribution + positive news that fails to produce a price recovery within the same session = immediate exit, no waiting for confirmation.
