# Investment Beliefs

## Confirm Before Sizing — Then Commit Without Hesitation [3]
Open scouts to test thesis conviction at low cost. When a scout gains 5%+ with OBV rising AND an external signal independently confirms the macro thesis (analyst upgrade, earnings beat, institutional options flow, major partnership announcement), upgrade to core immediately. The opportunity cost of hesitation on a confirmed winner exceeds the marginal risk of adding size. Conversely, never upgrade on price action alone — require the external confirmation signal as proof the market agrees with your thesis.

## Regime Change Entry Discipline [3]
When a macro regime change is being priced in (election, major policy shift, regulatory reset), identify sectors with direct policy exposure and rank candidates by RSI. Enter those with RSI below 75; avoid chasing those above 75. The sectors that have already repriced carry mean-reversion risk; the same theme at a reasonable RSI offers better risk-adjusted return. First-mover advantage applies to identifying THEMES early, not to chasing entry prices that have already fully moved.
