# Investment Themes

## AI Infrastructure Buildout [5]
Hyperscalers (Google, Meta, Microsoft, Amazon) are in a multi-year capex supercycle building AI data centers. Demand for GPUs, custom ASICs, networking chips, memory, and power infrastructure is accelerating. This is the dominant secular theme of 2024-2026. Primary beneficiaries: NVDA, AVGO, TSM, MU, ARM.

---

## Nuclear Renaissance [5]
AI data centers require massive, uninterrupted baseload power that renewables cannot reliably provide. Nuclear is the only clean, scalable solution. Utilities with nuclear capacity (CEG, VST) are signing long-term power purchase agreements with tech giants. This is a 3-5 year infrastructure theme that is already showing up in stock prices.

---

## Healthcare GLP-1 Supercycle [5]
GLP-1/GIP agonist drugs (tirzepatide, semaglutide) represent a generational pharmaceutical breakthrough addressing obesity, diabetes, and emerging cardiovascular/kidney indications. The US aging population with endemic obesity creates a decades-long demand runway. Market could exceed $100B annually. Primary beneficiaries: LLY, NVO.
