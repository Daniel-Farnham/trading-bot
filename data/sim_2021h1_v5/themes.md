# Investment Themes

## Blue Wave Policy Tailwinds [3]
Democrat control of Senate, House, and White House enables large fiscal stimulus ($1.9T+), infrastructure spending, and clean energy legislation. Creates a multi-year policy regime benefiting financials (rates), clean energy, and cyclicals. The political catalyst is now confirmed — this isn't speculation.

---

## Semiconductor Supercycle [4]
Global chip shortage emerging across autos, consumer electronics, and data centers. 5G device upgrades, AI inference demand, and cloud capital expenditure all require more silicon. TSMC is the indispensable chokepoint. This is a multi-year structural supply/demand imbalance.

---

## EV & Industrial Infrastructure [4]
Biden's EV mandates, federal fleet electrification, $2T infrastructure plan, and 'Buy American' provisions create a multi-year capex supercycle for industrial companies executing on electrification and infrastructure. This is distinct from the Clean Energy theme (which covers power generation/utilities) — this theme covers the physical hardware layer: EVs (Ford, GM), heavy equipment (Deere, Caterpillar), and industrial manufacturers pivoting to clean economy demand.
