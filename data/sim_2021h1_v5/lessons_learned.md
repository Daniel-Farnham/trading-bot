# Lessons Learned

## Lesson 1 [5]
Extreme ADX readings (>45) combined with OBV rising override overbought RSI signals. RSI=80+ in a stock with ADX=60 and OBV rising is NOT a sell signal — it is a trend confirmation signal. Standard overbought logic breaks down in regime-change momentum moves.

---

## Lesson 2 [4]
Earnings beats in supply-constrained industries are the strongest 'add to conviction' signals available. When a company at the structural chokepoint of a supercycle thesis (like TSM in semiconductor shortage) beats EPS AND provides above-estimate forward guidance, that is a first-principles confirmation the thesis is correct — not just technically, but fundamentally. This is the moment to add to the position or open related positions (MU, AVGO), not to take profits.

---

## Lesson 3 [3]
When multiple independent analyst firms reach consensus on a specific competitive timeline (e.g., 'Intel is 4 more years behind TSMC'), that sell-side consensus transforms a thesis from hypothesis to confirmed structural fact. Multi-analyst timeline consensus is the highest-quality thesis duration signal available — it de-risks extending position horizons and warrants adding to structural beneficiary positions (TSM, MU) rather than harvesting early gains.

---

## Lesson 4 [3]
Systemic market stress events (short squeeze blowups, forced hedge fund liquidation) cause OBV deterioration in fundamentally sound positions as institutions sell winners to meet margin calls. When OBV falls during a week dominated by a specific systemic event (GME, Lehman-type events), apply a 1-2 week lag before treating it as a thesis-breaking distribution signal. Check: is the OBV falling concentrated in one sector or broad-based? Broad-based OBV deterioration during a known systemic event is noise. Sector-specific OBV deterioration without a known systemic catalyst is signal.

## Lesson 5 [1]
Rate Sensitivity Is the Hidden Risk in 'Blue Wave' Clean Energy Utility Positions: Fiscal stimulus that drives long-end rates higher simultaneously benefits financials (NIM expansion) and HURTS high-duration clean energy utilities (discount rate compression on regulated returns). These two Blue Wave beneficiaries are not complementary — they are inverse rate bets. When opening clean energy utility positions in a fiscal stimulus environment, explicitly model the rate sensitivity and size accordingly. The trigger to apply this: any position in regulated utilities or yield-like clean energy assets opened during a period of rising deficit spending expectations. Lesson: treat rate sensitivity as an invalidation condition for utility-like clean energy positions when yield curve steepening is the concurrent macro theme.

## Lesson 6 [5]
When a position's ADX drops more than 20 points in a single review period AND crosses below the 35 exit threshold, apply a one-week watch window rather than immediate exit — unless OBV is confirmed falling for multiple consecutive weeks. The correct protocol: flag as WATCH, set a one-week deadline, and exit at the next review if ADX remains below 35 and OBV is still falling. This prevents premature exit on a single volatile week while enforcing discipline. Trigger condition: ADX drop >20 points in one review AND resulting ADX <35.

## Lesson 7 [1]
When a portfolio position's supply-constraint thesis is confirmed by NEGATIVE operational news in an adjacent downstream industry (e.g., Ford chip shortage plant shutdowns confirming semiconductor shortage), treat this as a thesis duration and scope extension signal — NOT a reason to exit. Adjacent industry pain proves the shortage is real, broad, and structurally persistent beyond the original thesis assumptions. The correct response is increased conviction in the supply-constrained position (MU, TSM), not reduced conviction. Trigger condition: portfolio company thesis tied to supply shortage receives confirmation via downstream industry production disruptions reported in news.

---

## Lesson 8 [3]
When a position at a loss shows ADX >30 AND OBV falling simultaneously, the exit rule is non-negotiable regardless of thesis validity. The thesis may be correct but the market is voting against it with institutional selling. Cutting a loss early when institutions are distributing (OBV falling into a loss) is structurally different from cutting during systemic stress (Lesson 4). Systemic stress causes broad-based OBV decline; thesis-specific OBV decline in a single losing position is the market telling you something the thesis has not yet acknowledged. Trigger: position negative P&L + ADX >30 + OBV falling + MACD bearish all present simultaneously.

---

## Lesson 9 [1]
When a position opened on an ADX >40 institutional regime entry shows ADX collapse >20 points to below 25 within 4-6 weeks AND a negative thesis-specific news catalyst emerges simultaneously (data breach, regulatory action, earnings miss), treat the combination as an immediate exit regardless of loss size. The ADX collapse indicates the institutional buyers who validated the entry thesis have already exited. The negative news catalyst confirms the thesis is broken, not temporarily disrupted. Do not wait for OBV to fall multiple consecutive weeks in this scenario — the ADX collapse plus thesis-specific catalyst is sufficient. Trigger: entry ADX >40 + current ADX <25 (>20 point drop) + negative thesis-specific news + OBV falling.
