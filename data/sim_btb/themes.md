Energy producers, commodity companies, and inflation-linked assets that outperform during Fed tightening cycles and supply-constrained commodity markets. Oil majors (XOM, CVX, OXY), natural gas (LNG), and select materials benefit from the same inflationary forces that hurt our growth/tech longs.
