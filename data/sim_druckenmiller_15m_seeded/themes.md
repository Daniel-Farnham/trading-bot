# Investment Themes

## AI Infrastructure Buildout [5]
Hyperscaler capex on AI compute, networking, and custom silicon driving multi-year spend cycle. Key beneficiaries: NVDA, AVGO, TSM, MU. Apple's iPhone 16 softness is consumer AI lag, not enterprise — hyperscaler AI spend remains intact.

---

## Nuclear Power Renaissance [5]
Data centers require 24/7 reliable baseload power that renewables cannot provide alone. Nuclear is being restarted and extended (Three Mile Island deal, etc). VST and CEG are direct beneficiaries. Multi-year theme as AI infrastructure expands.

---

## Crypto Deregulation Under Trump [5]
Trump's explicit pro-crypto platform represents a multi-year regulatory regime change for digital assets — from existential SEC enforcement threat to active government support. Trump named specific actions: replace Gensler, US crypto capital, Bitcoin strategic reserve. COIN is the primary equity instrument to capture this shift as the dominant regulated US exchange. Distinct from the broader Trump Trade theme in its specificity and magnitude.

---

## GLP-1 Drug Revolution [1]
Eli Lilly and Novo Nordisk are creating a generational pharmaceutical product cycle with GLP-1 agonists treating obesity, Type 2 diabetes, cardiovascular disease, sleep apnea, kidney disease, and potentially neurological conditions. The global obesity drug market alone is projected to exceed $100B by 2030. Multi-year secular growth as clinical data expands indications and global adoption increases.

---

## US Domestic Protectionism [3]
Liberation Day tariff regime creates durable structural advantages for domestic US producers and services. Direct beneficiaries: domestic energy producers (foreign oil tariffs support domestic pricing), domestic telecom infrastructure (tariff-immune lease revenues), domestic nuclear power generation (tariff-immune domestic operations), domestic financial services. The magnitude and breadth of the Liberation Day announcement signals a multi-year protectionist policy stance. This lens now applies to all position evaluations: domestic revenue model is a protective moat, global supply chain dependency is a liability. Overlaps with and reinforces Nuclear Renaissance and Crypto Deregulation themes.
