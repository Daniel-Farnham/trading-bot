# Investment Themes

## US LNG Export Boom [3]
Russian gas cutoff to Europe creates structural multi-year demand for US LNG exports. Trump administration will accelerate export terminal approvals and energy deals. Cheniere, New Fortress, and related energy infrastructure are primary beneficiaries. Europe is now permanently diversifying away from Russian supply.

---

## Power Demand / Nuclear Renaissance [3]
AI data centers require massive baseload power that renewables alone cannot reliably supply. Nuclear is uniquely positioned as clean, reliable, 24/7 baseload. Utilities with nuclear assets (VST, CEG) and power generation exposed to deregulated markets are repricing higher. This is a decade-long structural shift.

---

## Trump Deregulation / Energy Policy [5]
Trump's return to the White House signals fossil fuel expansion, rollback of clean energy mandates, financial deregulation, and potentially inflationary tariff policies. Oil & gas, financials, and defense benefit. Long-duration bonds face headwinds (Treasury ETF dump already underway). This creates a specific set of sector winners and losers.

---

## GLP-1 / Healthcare Innovation [4]
GLP-1 weight-loss drugs (Mounjaro, Ozempic) represent a structural multi-decade healthcare megatrend. 40%+ of US adults are obese, and drug penetration is in early innings. LLY and NVO are the primary beneficiaries of what may be the largest new drug category in history. Entirely independent of our macro/political themes — provides genuine portfolio diversification.
